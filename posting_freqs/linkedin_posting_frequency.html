KEY FINDINGS & CONTEXT

  • The "4x a week" rule is the minimum, not the goal. Out of the 16 highly visible posters selected, 14 exceeded 4 posts per week. Six post every single day—some dropping 2 to 3 posts daily.
  • High visibility is rarely manual. Over 60% of these creators (10 out of 16) show clear signs of using scheduling tools. They aren't typing in real-time; they are batch-creating and letting software do the heavy lifting.
  • Predictability is a superpower. Top creators treat their feed like a TV schedule. The most regimented posters drop their content at the exact same hour—sometimes down to the exact minute—every single day for months.
